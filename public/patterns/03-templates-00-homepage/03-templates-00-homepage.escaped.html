Many of them are meaningful windows into the fabric of both winemaking and winemakers and should be searchable in a way that is accessible.</p> <p>Before we can create a searchable database, we need to transcribe the labels. That’s where you come in! Help us uncork a piece of history.</p> </section> <section class="small-12 medium-12 large-10 large-offset-1 columns"> <h1>Here's how you can help:</h1> <!--Grid 3up--> <div class="row"> <div class="small-11 medium-expand columns step-by-step"> <h6>Step 1: Mark</h6> <img class="step-image" src="../../images/image-step-1.png" alt="Animated depiction of step 1: marking an area on a label"/> <p><small>Mark the areas on each label that will need transcribing or describing.</small></p> </div> <div class="small-11 medium-expand columns step-by-step"> <h6>Step 2: Transcribe</h6> <img class="step-image" src="../../images/image-step-2.png" alt="Animated depiction of step 2: transcribing some text on a label"/> <p><small>Transcribe the text that has been marked by other people.</small></p> </div> <div class="small-11 medium-expand columns step-by-step"> <h6>Step 3: Describe</h6> <img class="step-image" src="../../images/image-step-3.png" alt="Animated depiction of step 3: describing an image on a label"/> <p><small>Select a description for the images that have been marked by other people.</small></p> </div> </div> <!--End grid 2up--> </section> <section class="small-12 medium-10 medium-offset-1 columns"> <div class="row align-center"> <button type="button" class="large button" href="../../patterns/03-templates-02-mark-flow-start/03-templates-02-mark-flow-start.html"><img class="left-pointer" src="../../images/left-pointer-knockout.svg"/>Get Started<img class="right-pointer" src="../../images/right-pointer-knockout.svg"/></button> </div> </section> <section class="small-12 medium-10 medium-offset-1 large-8 large-offset-2 columns"> <h2>Just want to look at the pretty labels?</h2> <p>Since the labels aren’t transcribed yet, they’re just ordered randomly. That will help us organize how we present them to you.</p> <p>In the meantime, you can look through the labels at random below, or search for terms within the labels we’ve transcribed so far.</p> <ul
